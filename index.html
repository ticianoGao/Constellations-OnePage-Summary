The Constellations Report Generator

A K–12 Computer Science Resource Tool

Welcome to the Constellations Center Report Generator, a tool that allows users to examine schools, districts, and communities across the state of Georgia of their K–12 computer science resources. Once you access the dashboard or create the type of report you are interested in, you can review, download, export, and share your findings with others!

For optimal layout, the Constellations Report Generator is currently best viewed on a desktop/laptop.
