The Constellations Report Generator

A K–12 Computer Science Resource Tool

Text Example

Welcome to the Constellations Report Generator, a tool that allows users to examine how schools, districts, and communities across Georgia intersect with K–12 computer science access, course availability, and student participation. Once you access the dashboard or create the type of report you are interested in, you can review, download, export, and share your findings with others. Get started exploring below!

For optimal performance, the Constellations Report Generator is best viewed on a desktop or laptop.
